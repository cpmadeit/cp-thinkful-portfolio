This Is What I Do

As a freshman in college, I had no idea what I wanted to do for a career and even less of an idea on how to figure that out. I ended up pursuing degrees in History and Museum Studies and then working at Starbucks after graduation, still not knowing what I might like to do for a job. While the Covid-19 pandemic has been difficult and tragic in many ways, I took it as an opportunity to pause my current work life and really explore what I want to do and what might make me happy.

Since taking an HTML-focused computer class in highschool, I have always had an interest in coding and technology but never felt like it was a career option. At the beginning of the pandemic, while Starbucks' across the country were shutdown, I did some reasearch and took some free online courses that reinforced my interest in these areas and pushed me in the direction of a career move into a technology based field. In my research I found Thinkful. This program is allowing me to do something I never thought I would be able to do again. I am loving this opportunity to learn a new and challenging skillset in a field that I enjoy and aspire to use in the future to make some positive contribution to society.
